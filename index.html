What is this?

This is a my Backend solution to GDP Coding Exercise, wherein, I have been asked to develop a new checkout system for GDP. For details on what this is about, please refer - https://github.com/ChaturvediSulabh/seek-GDP-POS/README.md

Usage:

Route - https://gdp-checkout-microservice.herokuapp.com/users/checkout/

How to use? - Provide route with customer name and id's of classic, standout and premium ads.

What are the Id's for aforementioed ads ? - Classic = 1, standout = 2 and premium = 3
